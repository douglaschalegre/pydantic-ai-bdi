## What to build

Make reconsideration evaluate the active Plan as a whole instead of treating each small unit of work as a potentially complete Intention. Successful Plan Step progress should continue without normal reconsideration. Reconsideration should run after failures, contradictions, or stale assumptions and should return structured Plan-level outcomes.

## Acceptance criteria

- [ ] Successful Plan Step progress does not trigger normal reconsideration.
- [ ] Reconsideration receives completed Plan Steps, remaining Plan Steps, current Beliefs, and relevant failure history.
- [ ] Reconsideration evaluates Plan validity rather than isolated Plan Step sufficiency.
- [ ] Reconsideration returns structured outcomes that distinguish continue, repair Plan, replace Plan, and fail Desire.
- [ ] Tests cover success-path skip behavior and Plan-aware failure-path reconsideration.
